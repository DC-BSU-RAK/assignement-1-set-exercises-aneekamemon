Tiramisu - My Favorite Recipe

Tiramisu is an Italian delicacy, consisting of ladyfinger pastries stacked and layered through coffee dippings, glued together with a whipped and churned mixeture of egg yolks, sugar, and mascarpone. Topped off with cocoa powder.

The Ingredients list:

  • Ladyfingers
  • Mascarpone
  • Coffee
  • Heavy Whipped Cream
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Vanilla extract
  • Cocoa powder

Here's how to make Tiramisu:

  1. Mix the mascarpone, whipped cream, granulated sugar, and vanilla together until it becomes frothy.
  2. Grab a shallow bowl and pour in some simple espresso and dip the Ladyfingers, coat both the sides with the coffee (do not soak, but rather just dip).
  3. Add a layer of mascarpone and whipped cream on top of the now dipped in coffee ladyfingers, which should start to structure the tirasmisu.
  4. Repeat the first 3 steps thrice, and dust the final tirasmisu structure with some cocoa powder.
  5. Enjoy some delicious tirasmisu!
